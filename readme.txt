Appends the customizable multi-lingual SharePost bookmarking or feed-subscription widget to posts.

== Description ==

**Get Noticed Worldwide**

Create a customized sharing widget in many different languages and help your international visitors 
share your content and spread the word to their friends.

The SharePost widget integrates with many international social bookmarking services, so your visitors 
are offered sharing services with which they’re most familiar to help them share your content with their 
friends and others that speak the same language. This can help you gain international interest and more 
traffic from a broader audience.

**Customize It!**

There are many ways to customize the look of the SharePost widget. Change buttons, colors, sharing tools, 
and languages. It’s easy to integrate the SharePost widget into your website or blog.

By presenting visitors with an appealing sharing tool in their languages, you increase the likelihood of 
your content being shared with others.

* Choose a sharing button.
* Set custom colors.
* Choose different services to present to your visitors.
* Customize the language.

**Free Comprehensive Analytics**

With SharePost, you can help your site visitors share your content with a vast array of popular social 
bookmarking services. But tracking this sharing also becomes important to help you gain insight into 
the popularity of your content.

SharePost provides a powerful set of analytics for free to help you get the most out of the analysis 
of your traffic and the sharing that takes place. You not only get a birds-eye view of your traffic 
and the tools used to share, but you can also get useful details on the most common systems and 
browsers used, the regions from where you get the most traffic, and the most common sources of 
traffic to your website or blog.

Visit [SharePost](http://www.sharepost.com/ "SharePost") for more information.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the sharepost plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Click on 'SharePost' in the Settings menu in WordPress
1. On the 'Plugin Settings' tab, sign in to your SharePost account, or sign up for a new one
1. Use the controls on the other tabs to customize your widget

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will this plugin work with older versions of WordPress? =

The plugin was developed on WordPress 2.7.1.  It may work on older versions, but has not been tested.
